About team eques

Our Mission

To create a world where every teammate, horse or human, is released of limitations and empowered to achieve.

Our Vision

To produce top quality competition horses and riders without compromising ones physical, mental or emotional health. We believe in developing our teammates relative to their individual strengths and abilities, rather than conforming to an expected learning curve.

Our Core Values

The foundation which cements all relationships is build on TRUST. Therefore, our Team vows to Respect, Unite, Serve and Transform.
