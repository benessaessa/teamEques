RIDER FITNESS

DIGITAL SOLUTIONS

EqueStride TM

EqueStride is our mobile app solution for the busy equestrian seeking to improve their fitness. Designed in a manner that will allow users to understand and challenge their fitness level, without negatively impacting their ride, means equestrians can safely improve their abilities. Exhausting our bodies in the gym can lead to the inability to engage our muscles, ligaments, and tendons properly while on board our horses, Download EqueStride today to see how you can challenge your body and improve your ride

The Four Programs

EqueStculpt: A Fluid combination of isolating movements necessary to evolove your individual balance and endurance.
EqueStrength: Improve the power of your routine while challenging your deepest core strength.
EqueStretch: A pattern of reach and release used to unwind and enable your flexibility.
EqueCore: challenge even the smallest supporting muscle groups enabling the most secure connection to your horse

ON SITE FITNESS

All four EqueStride Programs are available in person on a one-to-one basis or in agroup setting at the Team Eques Facility. Space is limited and must be booked in advance.
