Our Team

Those who choose Team Eques - are Team Eques!

Whether you’re a client, livery, employee, student, or professional associate, we believe in the power and strength of group commitment. We are only as successful as those around us – and we thrive on celebrating success. By joining our team, you will be joining a group of passionate equestrians who believe in ensuring the focus and care for both horse and rider delivers exceptional physical, mental, and emotional health. By developing the members of our team through these focus points, we can be confident in the product we deliver both in and outside of the arena. The combination of these three points are brought forth through every decision we make to guarantee the best life is led for any human or horse. Whether Grand Prix aspirations are within your sights, or you are simply searching for an escape from city life aboard your equestrian companion, we are here to deliver the expertise and atmosphere you seek
